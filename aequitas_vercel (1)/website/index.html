Academic Rigour

Not a toy.
Not a demo.

Every model cites its primary source. No approximations where exact solutions exist. Numerical guards on every computation. Tested against Bloomberg and published academic results.

◈
Maximum Likelihood Estimation
GARCH parameters are estimated via Nelder-Mead MLE with multiple random restarts to avoid local optima. Identical methodology to RiskMetrics and MSCI Barra.
◉
Exact Closed-Form Pricing
Heston uses the Gil-Pelaez characteristic function inversion — exact prices, not Monte Carlo approximations. SABR uses the Hagan (2002) asymptotic expansion with no truncation error.
◎
Ledoit-Wolf Regularisation
Portfolio covariance matrices use the Ledoit-Wolf shrinkage estimator and nearest positive-definite projection. Avoids the estimation error explosion that breaks naive Markowitz with 20+ assets.
⊕
Institutional Data Construction
Fama-French factors are constructed from live ETF returns (SPY, IWM, IVE, IVW) net of the 3-month T-bill rate — the same methodology used by major quant desks when the Ken French library has lag.
