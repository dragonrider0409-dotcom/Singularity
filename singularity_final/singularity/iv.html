SABR (Hagan 2002)
dF = α·Fβ·dWF
dα = ν·α·dWα
dWF·dWα = ρ·dt

α: initial vol (ATM vol level)
β: CEV exponent (0=normal, 1=lognormal)
ρ: F–vol correlation (skew driver)
ν: vol-of-vol (smile curvature)

HESTON (1993)
dS = μS·dt + √v·S·dWS
dv = κ(θ−v)·dt + ξ√v·dWv
dWS·dWv = ρ·dt

v₀: initial variance
κ: mean-reversion speed
θ: long-run variance
ξ: vol-of-vol
ρ: S–vol correlation

Priced via characteristic function
integration (exact, no MC noise).
Feller condition: 2κθ > ξ²
