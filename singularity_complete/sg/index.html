Capabilities
Built for the real market
📡
Live Market Data
All dashboards pull real-time prices via Yahoo Finance. The Trading Bot fetches live intraday volume every 3 minutes to rank which instruments have the highest activity and trains hardest on those.
🤖
Autonomous Training Bot
Trains across 4 global sessions: New York (US equities), London (forex + UK), Tokyo (JPY pairs + Japanese equities), Hong Kong (AUD/CNH + crypto). Overdrive mode activates when two sessions overlap — all 20 instruments training simultaneously.
🔮
Ghost Candle Predictions
5 distinct Monte Carlo scenarios rendered as translucent OHLC candles on the live chart. Each scenario detects the dominant candle pattern (engulfing, doji, hammer, shooting star), calculates SL/TP from ATR, and shows the R:R ratio. Focus boxes show where the bot is watching.
🏦
Interactive Brokers Integration
Paper trading on port 7497 — IBKR's own simulator, no real money. Switch to live (port 7496) after validation. The included Python bridge script (singularity_ib_bridge.py) handles order routing. Manual order entry with Market, Limit, and Stop types. TradingView webhook also supported.
